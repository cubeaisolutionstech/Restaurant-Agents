MML Restaurant Booking System Test

This is a simple test to verify the booking flow:

Expected Conversation Flow:

  1. System: "Hello and welcome to MML Restaurant! I'm your voice reservation assistant. To book your table, may I please have your name?"
  2. User: "My name is John Smith"
  3. System: "Perfect, John Smith! Now, what date would you like to make your reservation?"
  4. User: "Tomorrow"
  5. System: "Great! Tomorrow it is. What time would you prefer for your reservation?"
  6. User: "7 PM"
  7. System: "Excellent! 7:00 PM on Tomorrow. How many people will be joining you for dinner?"
  8. User: "Four people"
  9. System: "Perfect! Table for 4 people. Do you have any special requests?"
  10. User: "Window seat please"
  11. System: "Let me confirm your reservation at MML Restaurant: John Smith for 4 people on Tomorrow at 7:00 PM. Special requests: Window seat please. Is this correct?"
  12. User: "Yes"
  13. System: "Wonderful! Your reservation is confirmed. We'll see you at MML Restaurant on Tomorrow at 7:00 PM."
  14. User: "Thank you"
  15. System: "Thank you for choosing MML Restaurant! Your reservation is confirmed. We look forward to serving you. Have a wonderful day! Goodbye!" (Call ends automatically)

Key Features Implemented:

  • ✅ Sequential question flow (Name → Date → Time → Members → Special Requests)
  • ✅ MML Restaurant branding throughout
  • ✅ Voice extraction for names, dates, times, and member counts
  • ✅ Automatic call termination on "thank you"
  • ✅ Confirmation step before finalizing reservation
  • ✅ Natural language processing for common responses
